Understanding GST Inclusive vs Exclusive

GST Exclusive: Calculated when you have a base catalog item value and need to add tax to compute the final bill value. The formula adds tax as: Amount × (Rate / 100).

GST Inclusive: Calculated when the total price paid already includes tax (such as standard consumer items), and you want to extract the underlying base price. The base formula is: Gross Value / (1 + (Rate / 100)).

Standard Tax Rate Slabs in India

India enforces 5 main percentage slabs:

  • 3%: Gold, precious stones, jewellery.
  • 5%: Essential goods, packaged foodstuffs.
  • 12%: Processed foods, business services, mobile phones.
  • 18%: IT consulting, software services, restaurants, luxury items.
  • 28%: Cars, cement, high-luxury consumables.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are CGST, SGST, and IGST?
CGST (Central GST) and SGST (State GST) are levied on sales occurring inside the same state (intrastate transactions), splitting the tax revenue equally. IGST (Integrated GST) is charged for interstate commerce (sales between states), with revenue collected by the Central Government.
Are custom rates supported?
Yes. If you deal with specific items outside the standard grids, simply type your percentage into the "Custom Rate" input box to calculate instantly.
Is the calculations data stored anywhere?
No. All calculations are executed dynamically in your local JavaScript interpreter inside your browser. No cookies, session records, or servers are involved.
