Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between an Invoice and a Quotation?
A quotation is an initial proposed price estimate sent to clients for project negotiation and approval. An invoice is issued after the services have been rendered or contract completed, demanding payment.
How does the "Copy Share Link" feature work?
When you click "Copy Share Link", all your filled inputs (client name, items, pricing structure, logo excluded for length) are serialized into a safe web link. When a client opens that link, the quotation builder renders your quotation precisely as designed.
Is my information private?
Absolutely. Like all Kyro tools, all processing, inputs, and PDF generations occur directly in your local browser sandbox. No server writes are performed.
